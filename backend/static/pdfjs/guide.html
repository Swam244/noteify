Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the browser extension?

Visit our extension store page and click "Add to Browser". Follow the prompts to complete installation.

Can I use Noteify without Notion?

Currently, Notion integration is required for storing and organizing your notes. We're working on additional storage options.

How does the AI categorization work?

Our AI analyzes the content of your notes and automatically assigns relevant categories and tags based on the subject matter.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Can I export my notes?

Yes, you can export your notes in various formats including PDF, Markdown, and plain text from your dashboard.

How do I capture content from websites?

Simply highlight any text, image, or code snippet on a webpage and click the Noteify icon or use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+Shift+N.

Troubleshooting

Common issues and their solutions

Extension not working on some websites

Some websites block extensions for security. Try refreshing the page or check if the website allows extensions. Corporate firewalls may also block the extension.

Common Issue

Notes not syncing to Notion

Check your Notion connection in Settings. Ensure you have proper permissions and that your Notion workspace is accessible. Try disconnecting and reconnecting.

Common Issue

Extension not working on my browser (e.g., Firefox, Safari)

Currently, Noteify is only available for Chromium-based browsers. We are working on expanding support to other browsers in the future!
